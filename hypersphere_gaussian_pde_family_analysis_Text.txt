This script is used to analyse how well the train VAE has learned the space of PDEs by sampling from the latent space from using two methods.

Main Functionality:
1. Loads the trained VAE model and dataset and converts the dataset into binary structure only.
2. Computes all the theoretical PDEs through enumeration across all the possible binary candidates.
3. The latent space is then sampled using Gaussian and Uniform ball sampling and these samples are decoded into PDE structures.
4. Finally analysis is carried out by this script to determine:
	(i) How many of the training PDE structures were rediscovered in the samples,
	(ii) Determines the number of novel structures found from sampling that were not present in the training dataset,
	(iii) Determines the number of samples that obey the constrains imposed on the dataset,
	(iv) Gives a breakdown of the number of each defined PDE family/group present within the admissible samples,
	(v) Compares the generated PDEs to the theoretical PDEs to determine if the VAE is capable of generalisation.

Outputs:
1. Writes any missing and novel PDEs to text files and compares the learned latent space to the theoretical space.

Requirements:
1. PyTorch
2. NumPy
