This script defines how the original candidate of 15 million+ PDE structures were generated, introducing sparsity and relationships between derivative orders.

Main Functionality:
1. Generates PDE structures with each column representing an indexed derivative order.
2. Controls the number of linear and nonlinear terms and introduces sparsity amongst the equations
3. Ensures uniqueness by removing any duplicates

Outputs:
1. Creates the candidate library of PDE binary structures with limited constraints, providing structural data for filtering.

Requirements:
1. Numpy
